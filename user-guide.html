DueDock User Guide

Welcome to DueDock! This comprehensive guide will help you master DueDock's features for effective bill management and financial planning.

Getting Started

1. Initial Setup

Step 1: Open DueDock and grant access to your Files app for backup/export features.
Step 2: Create your first Account to organize bills by bank, credit card, or category.
Step 3: Add your first bill using the + button.

2. Understanding the Dashboard

The dashboard shows your financial overview with color-coded indicators:

  • Green: Healthy coverage - you have enough funds
  • Yellow: Caution - tight budget, review spending
  • Red: Alert - insufficient funds for upcoming bills

Core Features

Coverage Check

The Coverage Check automatically shows what's due before your next income source. It's always active and updates in real-time.

Pro Tip: Use Coverage Check for day-to-day planning and the Planning Period for longer-term goals.

Planning Period

Set a future end date to see your financial summary for any time period. Perfect for:

  • Monthly budget planning
  • Vacation cost calculations
  • Savings goal tracking

Bill Management

Adding Bills

To add a bill:
1. Tap the + button
2. Enter bill name and amount
3. Set due date and frequency
4. Select account and category
5. Add tags for organization (optional)

Docking Bills

"Docking" marks a bill as paid and advances recurring bills to their next due date:

  • One-time bills: Marked as paid and archived
  • Recurring bills: Due date automatically advances
  • Monthly bills: Same date next month
  • Weekly bills: Same day next week

Advanced Features

For bills with changing amounts (credit cards, utilities):

Update Methods:

Payment Conflicts

DueDock automatically detects when multiple bills are due on the same day and helps you prioritize:

  • Red indicator shows conflict days
  • Tap to see which bills conflict
  • Adjust due dates or set up partial payments

Partial Payments & Installments

Important: Partial payments and installments require careful tracking to avoid missed payments.

Partial Payments: Pay part of a bill now, the rest later

Installments: Break large bills into equal smaller payments

Organization Features

Accounts

Organize bills by financial source:

  • Bank accounts (checking, savings)
  • Credit cards
  • Digital wallets
  • Categories (utilities, subscriptions, etc.)

Tags

Use tags to group related bills:

  • #home - Mortgage, rent, utilities
  • #transport - Car payment, insurance, gas
  • #subscriptions - Netflix, Spotify, software

Savings Goals

Track long-term savings targets separate from bills:

  • Always visible on dashboard
  • Progress tracking with visual indicators
  • Target dates and contribution planning

Tools & Utilities

Calendar View

Visual calendar shows all due dates and income sources:

  • Color-coded by bill status
  • Tap any date for details
  • Month and year navigation

Backup & Export

Two backup options available:

Autosave: Automatic backup to DueDockAutosave folder

  • Automatic after changes
  • Deleted if app is uninstalled
  • Good for temporary recovery

Export: Manual backup to chosen location

  • Permanent backup
  • Store safely (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.)
  • Essential for data preservation

Undo System

DueDock includes comprehensive undo for most actions:

  • Bill deletions (30-day window)
  • Docked bills
  • Amount changes
  • Account modifications

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Issue: "Can't delete Account"
Solution: Re-assign or delete all bills first
Issue: "Bill not showing in Coverage Check"
Solution: Check due date is after today and before next income
Issue: "Backup not working"
Solution: Grant Files app access in Settings

Performance Tips

  • Keep bill list under 100 items for optimal performance
  • Use tags instead of many accounts for organization
  • Regularly archive paid bills older than 6 months

Best Practices

Daily Routine: Check Coverage Check each morning to see what's due today.
Weekly Review: Use Planning Period to review the upcoming week.
Monthly Planning: Set Planning Period to end of month for budget review.

Privacy & Security

DueDock is designed with privacy as the top priority:

  • 100% Offline: No internet connection required
  • Local Storage: All data stored on your device
  • Private iCloud Sync: Encrypted sync to your personal iCloud
  • No Analytics: No tracking or data collection
  • No Bank Connections: Manual entry only
Remember: Your financial data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.
